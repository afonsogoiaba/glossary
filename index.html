Chapter 1: The start!

Language is something important in every way, because without it the human being would not be able to think. The knowledge of the most varied sciences comes from the study of language. this is the beginning of everything and for this to happen, it is necessary to study as words.

Chapter 2: Why study?

Today there are many languages ​​on the planet. English is one of the most spoken languages ​​on the planet, being present in 60 countries of the world. The study of language, therefore, becomes important for the communication of the human being in the most varied situations.

Why did we do this?

We students know how difficult it is when we do not understand a subject.That's why we came up with the idea of ​​creating a glossary that will help online students with English difficulties.We know that for some people it is difficult to learn another language, however, to help these language adventurers, we have created a glossary loaded with words to be studied.Let's see how this works!
